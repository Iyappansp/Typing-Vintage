Common Questions
Do you ship internationally?
Yes — we ship to over 40 countries via our museum-grade custom crating service. International shipping typically takes 7–14 business days with full insurance coverage.
Can I send my typewriter for restoration?
Absolutely. We accept machines for restoration from anywhere in the world. We'll provide shipping instructions and a pre-paid return label once we've assessed your submission.
How long does a full restoration take?
Most full restorations take 3–6 weeks from intake to dispatch, depending on parts availability and our current workshop queue. We'll give you a precise estimate after initial assessment.
Do you buy or consign machines?
Yes to both. We actively seek interesting machines to add to our collection or sell on consignment. Email us with photos and we'll typically respond within 24 hours.
